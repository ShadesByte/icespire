The frontier town at the center of the campaign, living in the shadow of the
white dragon rumors.

**Known locations:**

- **The quest board & Quest Master** — where the work comes from.
- **Harbin Wester's house** — the townmaster pays out here, behind many locks.
- **The inn** — limited gnome intel, and at least one Zhentarim watcher among
  the patrons.
- **The Miner's Exchange** — run by Guildmaster Holia Thornton, who is more
  than she appears.

**Regional concerns:** rising orc activity (Adabra's warning, the tracks and
encampment near the excavation), and the white dragon everyone keeps sending
warnings about. Phandalin's healing potion supply depends entirely on Adabra
Gwynn's windmill at Umbrage Hill.
