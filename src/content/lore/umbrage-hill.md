A stone windmill outside Phandalin, home and workshop of midwife-apothecary
**Adabra Gwynn**, who brews the healing potions supplied to the town.

The party arrived on Harbin's contract to bring Adabra to safety and instead
found **two manticores** tearing the place apart (Session 4). After a long,
scrappy fight — fog cloud, magic missiles, handaxes, sneak attacks, an Action
Surge — Bean and Sage put the manticores down.

The windmill survives. Mostly. Adabra is staying: she can't brew anywhere
else, and the town needs the potions. The party promised to check on her on
the return trip.
