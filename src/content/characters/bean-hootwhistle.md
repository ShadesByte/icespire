Bean Hootwhistle is small, cheerful, and extremely good at ending fights and
acquiring other people's valuables.

## Notable deeds

- Out like a light on the party's first watch, then bitten by the boar that
  wandered into camp (Session 1).
- Found Facktoré's bag of 10 gold — her favorite! (Session 2).
- Fought and beat two mimics with the party in the Gnomengarde storage room
  (Session 3).
- Lifted Harbin Wester's private notes on Guildmaster Thornton during an
  unauthorized return visit to his house (Session 4).
- Landed the kill on the first manticore at Umbrage Hill (Session 4).
- Crept into the dwarven excavation to scout, kicked a pebble, stayed unseen
  anyway — and then announced herself: "I'm Bean!" (Session 5).
- Landed a natural-20 sneak attack in the ochre jelly fight (Session 5).
