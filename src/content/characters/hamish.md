One of the party's two wood elf rangers, Hamish handles the scouting, the
tracking, and — when the longbow isn't enough — the leaping-in-with-a-shortsword.

## Notable deeds

- Spotted the wild boar on second watch the party's first night out (Session 1).
- Took an interest in the mushrooms growing in the Gnomengarde pond and learned
  how to harvest them from the gnome farmers (Session 2).
- Helped Sage partially disable Facktoré's crossbow ballista (Session 2).
- Recognized Guildmaster Thornton's dragon pin as the mark of the Zhentarim
  (Session 4).
- Scouted ahead with Sage and confirmed the large orc encampment down the split
  trail (Session 5).
- Poured longbow fire into the original wall jelly all fight, then leaped in
  with his shortsword for the final kill (Session 5).
- Found two secret doors in the temple's main chamber, then went to investigate
  a glint in the rubble — which turned out to be one more ochre jelly (Session 5).
