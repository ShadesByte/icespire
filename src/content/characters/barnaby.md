The party's dragonborn paladin: heavy hitter, healer of last resort, and the
one who ends up carrying people (and tied-up gnomes) when things go sideways.

## Notable deeds

- Hit the wild boar with Dragon's Breath for substantial damage (Session 1).
- Carried Facktoré and Bean to the Gnomengarde entrance after the ballista
  incident (Session 2).
- Burned his reaction to shield Dax mid-fight against the manticores at
  Umbrage Hill (Session 4).
- Used Lay on Hands to get the badly wounded party back on their feet after
  the manticore fight (Session 5).
- His read of the orc movements gave the party the confidence to push on to the
  excavation (Session 5).
- Blessed the front line in the ochre jelly fight (Session 5).
