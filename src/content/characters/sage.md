The party's other wood elf ranger. Sage would rather calm an animal than kill
it — but when it comes to it, she doesn't miss.

## Notable deeds

- Tried to soothe the wild boar for a close-up attack, failed, and reluctantly
  landed the killing blow with an arrow (Session 1).
- Helped Hamish partially disable Facktoré's crossbow ballista (Session 2).
- Put an arrow through the second manticore's eye to end the Umbrage Hill fight
  (Session 4).
- Became the party's liaison to Adabra Gwynn, and carried her note back for
  Harbin confirming she's staying put (Session 5).
- Scouted ahead with Hamish and confirmed the large orc encampment (Session 5).
- Went in close with dual daggers to finish a split ochre jelly (Session 5).
- Read the Abbathor necklace's inscription aloud: *"This is what happens to
  those who displease me."* (Session 5).
