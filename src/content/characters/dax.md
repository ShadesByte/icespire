The party found Dax in Session 2, wounded amid five dead orcs on a parallel
contract to Gnomengarde. He joined cautiously — unsure at first whether the
group was the fastest way to finish his quest — and stayed because the jobs
keep paying.

With his original trade contract discharged (the Guildmaster's contract
delivered to King Korboz, the King's sealed envelope delivered back to
Guildmaster Thornton), Dax works wherever the coin is: Harbin's contracts, and
Thornton's standing offer of payment for dragon intelligence. He's the party's
contractual link to the Zhentarim guildmaster — though he sat out Bean's
unauthorized visit to Harbin's house, and doesn't know what her notes say.

## Notable deeds

- Tied up Facktoré after Thom put her to sleep (Session 2).
- Delivered the Guildmaster's contract to King Korboz and carried the King's
  sealed envelope back to Phandalin (Sessions 3–4).
- Burned an Action Surge in the manticore fight at Umbrage Hill; Barnaby
  shielded him with a reaction (Session 4).
- Tossed Adabra's free healing potion to a still-bleeding Thom (Session 5).
- Attempted a strategic read of the orc movements from his military days and
  came up with nothing ("Wasn't in the military very long anyway") (Session 5).
- Earned the dwarf prospectors' goodwill with a formal introduction in Dwarvish
  (Session 5).
- Discovered the hidden coffer of gemstones in a pillar base — the dwarves
  don't know (Session 5).
- Carries the Clockwork Amulet.

**Tactical note:** against ochre jellies, thrown handaxes and bludgeoning work;
slashing just makes more jellies. His longsword follow-up after a
jelly-splitting handaxe proved this the hard way. Lead with thrown weapons.
