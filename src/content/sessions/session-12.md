The party was halfway up the mountain trail when the temperature dropped twenty
degrees in the space of a breath. Wrenna noticed the shadow first. Everyone else
noticed the shadow second, because the shadow was the size of a barn.

Cryovain did not attack. She circled twice, low enough that the party could count
her teeth, and then went back to the peak. Cormund later described this as "a
territorial display consistent with juvenile dragon behavior." Kettlebrew described
it as "rude."
