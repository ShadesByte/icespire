The players met on the road to Phandalin, where Thom made a "friend" named
"Friend."

In town, the party talked to a townsperson, checked the quest board, spoke with
the Quest Master, and visited the inn. Choosing the **Gnomengarde Quest** —
warn the gnomes about the dragon, with a bonus for bringing back any magical
objects — earned them the Quest Master's first mention of dragon rumors. The
innkeeper offered limited intel on the gnomes (tinkerers, reclusive), and the
party noticed a strange person watching them at the inn. Nothing further at
the time — but remember him.

They traveled toward Gnomengarde and camped overnight a few miles out. Sage
took first shift; Bean was out like a light; Thom and "Friend" enjoyed their
mulled earth. On second shift, Hamish noticed a wild boar, attacked, and
missed. The boar ran into camp, where Bean tried attacking, missed, and took
damage for her trouble. Barnaby hit it with Dragon's Breath for substantial
damage; Sage tried to soothe the boar for a close-up attack (failed), then
reluctantly landed the killing blow with an arrow.

The party preserved the boar for food and finished their long rest.

*Play resumes the next morning.*
