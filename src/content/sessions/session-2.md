The party woke and prepared bacon for breakfast. Rut seemed interested in
Barnaby and got in his face.

After burying the boar, the party met **Dax**, a wounded fighter on a similar
quest toward Gnomengarde, surrounded by five dead orcs and carrying orc rumors
of a dragon in the area. Given food, he cautiously joined the party toward
their shared destination — unsure whether joining the group was actually the
fastest way to complete his quest.

Inside Gnomengarde, Hamish took an interest in mushrooms growing in the pond
and discovered they were being farmed. The party wandered the passageways and
found crab-like contraptions sized perfectly for Rut and Bean, then reached
the kitchen, where gnomes were preparing mushrooms into oil, bread, and wine.
Rut stole a piece of bread; Hamish learned how to harvest mushrooms. The baker
told Dax to find the king to complete his quest, kicked Thom in the shins for
asking too many questions (a busy woman, after all), and offered a tip to
reach the king faster — which the party didn't trust.

Then the party met **Facktoré**, a crazed gnome eager to test her heavy
crossbow ballista on new targets. She immediately shot Thom. Sage and Hamish
partially disabled the machine, Thom cast Sleep on the gnome, and Dax tied her
up. Bean found her bag of 10 gold — her favorite!

Barnaby carried Facktoré and Bean to the entrance they'd been directed to and
were met by several gnomes, less concerned about Thom's bleeding and more
worried about their crazy friend all tied up. Poor Facktoré! During a heated
argument about letting a lunatic operate heavy weaponry, Rut freed her — and
she promptly screeched and ran away.
