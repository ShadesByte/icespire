Co-ruler of Gnomengarde with King Korboz. The party first saw her through a
peephole into the King's chambers — tied up while the mimic-paranoid King
checked everything in the room by hand (Session 3). Once the King's madness was
resolved, he untied her, and the two resumed ruling Gnomengarde together.
