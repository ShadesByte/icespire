Guildmaster of Phandalin's Miner's Exchange — and, as her dragon-symbol pin
revealed, an agent of the **Zhentarim**. Dax's former contract employer.

- Received the King's sealed letter from Dax, closing out his original trade
  contract (Session 4).
- The letter's mention of the dragon prompted pointed questions; then came the
  pin, the crossbows, and a forced information-sharing agreement — the party
  keeps her informed on dragon intelligence, and she pays for updates
  (Session 4).
- Her guard outside is the same strange figure who watched the party at the
  inn back in Session 1.
- Let slip a lead: a ruined temple south along the **Trivial Trail**, where
  locals stash their gold (Session 4).
- Harbin keeps private notes on her — notes Bean now has.
