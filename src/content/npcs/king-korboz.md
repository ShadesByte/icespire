Co-ruler of Gnomengarde with Queen Gnerkli. When the party found him he was
touching everything in his chambers, paranoid that any of it might be a mimic
— with the Queen tied up nearby. Given that the party then fought two actual
mimics in the storage room, his paranoia was not entirely unfounded.

- Received the Guildmaster's contract from the party (Session 3).
- Gave Dax a sealed envelope — proof of the completed trade — later delivered
  to Guildmaster Thornton in Phandalin (Sessions 3–4).
