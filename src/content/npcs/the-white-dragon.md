The threat hanging over the whole region. The party has never laid eyes on it
— but everyone else seems to know it's coming:

- The Quest Master's dragon rumors sent the party to warn Gnomengarde
  (Session 1).
- Dax's orc rumors said the same thing (Session 2).
- Harbin's Dwarven Excavation contract exists to warn the prospectors that a
  white dragon has moved into the area (Session 4).
- Guildmaster Thornton pays standing coin for any dragon intelligence
  (Session 4).

Sooner or later, the warnings run out and the dragon shows up.
