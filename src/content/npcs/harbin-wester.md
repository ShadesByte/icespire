Phandalin's townmaster and the party's main quest-giver. Unlocks his door
through a comical series of bolts, scans the sky for wings, and hurries
visitors inside.

- Paid 135 gold (19 each) for the Gnomengarde job and returned the wand
  (Session 4).
- Posted the **Umbrage Hill** (25 gp) and **Dwarven Excavation** (50 gp)
  contracts (Session 4).
- Keeps private notes on Guildmaster Thornton — which Bean lifted during an
  unauthorized return visit. He doesn't know (Session 4).
- Adabra Gwynn knows him well; Sage carries her note back to him confirming
  she's staying at her windmill (Session 5).
