A Colletite of Chauntea who lives and works in the stone windmill at Umbrage
Hill — the one the party saved from two manticores.

- Refused Harbin's request to relocate: she insists she can't brew anywhere
  but her windmill, and her potion supply matters to the town (Session 5).
- Gave the party a free healing potion (Dax tossed it to a bleeding Thom) and
  a home-cooked meal (Session 5).
- Knows Harbin well; sent a note back with Sage confirming she's staying put
  (Session 5).
- Warned that orcs have grown more common in the area lately.
- The party agreed to check on her on the return trip.
