A crazed Gnomengarde gnome, eager to test her heavy crossbow ballista on new
targets — starting with Thom (Session 2).

Sage and Hamish partially disabled the machine, Thom put her to sleep, Dax
tied her up, and Bean pocketed her bag of 10 gold. The other gnomes were more
worried about their tied-up friend than about Thom's bleeding — poor Facktoré!
During the heated argument about letting a lunatic operate heavy weaponry, Rut
freed her, and she promptly screeched and ran away.

She has not been seen since.
