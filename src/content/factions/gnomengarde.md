A reclusive community of gnome tinkerers and mushroom-farmers, ruled by
**King Korboz** and **Queen Gnerkli**. Known for crab-like contraptions,
mushroom oil, bread, and wine — and, briefly, a mimic problem.

## Dealings with the party

- The party's first contract: warn Gnomengarde about the dragon, with a bonus
  for bringing back magic items (Sessions 1–3).
- The party cleared **two mimics** from the storage room, vindicating the
  King's paranoia (Session 3).
- The Guildmaster's trade contract was delivered to the King, who sealed his
  reply in an envelope entrusted to Dax (Session 3).
- Casualties of the visit: Thom's shins (the baker), Thom's torso (Facktoré's
  ballista), and the party's standing with the gnomes who found their friend
  Facktoré tied up.
