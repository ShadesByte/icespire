An unscrupulous shadow network expanding its influence across Faerûn —
ambitious, opportunistic, and meritocratic. Publicly a mercenary and
protection outfit; secretly running smuggling, extortion, and worse.

## The party's entanglement

- **Guildmaster Holia Thornton** of Phandalin's Miner's Exchange wears the
  Zhentarim dragon pin. Hamish recognized the mark (Session 4).
- Her guard — the strange figure who watched the party at the inn back in
  Session 1 — is one of her people. The Network had eyes on the party from
  their first night in town.
- At crossbow-point, Thornton pressed the party into an **information-sharing
  pact**: all dragon intelligence flows to her, and she pays for updates
  (Session 4).
- Dax, whose original contract was with Thornton, remains pragmatically
  willing to keep working with her so long as coin is on the table.
- Bean holds Harbin's stolen private notes on Thornton — contents so far
  unexamined at the table.
