A stone windmill on a hill southeast of Phandalin, home of the midwife and
apothecary Adabra Gwynn — who brews the town's healing potions and refuses to
leave, dragon or no dragon. The party fought off two manticores here in
Session 4 and left with a free healing potion and a promise to check in on
the way back.
