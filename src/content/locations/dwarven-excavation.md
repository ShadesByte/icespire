A ruined settlement behind a broken black-stone gate, where dwarf prospectors
Norbus Ithermel and Dazlyn Grayshard are digging into a buried temple of
Abbathor — and where the god's transformed priests, now ochre jellies, block
the dig. Site of the 60/40 treasure split, the hidden gemstone coffer, and
the necklace that reads *"This is what happens to those who displease me."*
