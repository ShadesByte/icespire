The party's overnight stop on the road between Phandalin and Gnomengarde.
Barnaby's Dragon's Breath and Sage's reluctant finishing arrow brought down a
wild boar here in Session 1. Nearby, in Session 2, the party found Dax —
wounded, surrounded by five dead orcs, and open to persuasion.
