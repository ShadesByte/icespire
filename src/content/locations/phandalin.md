The party's home base — a frontier town of farmers and prospectors where
every job so far has started. Harbin Wester posts contracts from behind a
well-bolted door, the Miner's Exchange hides a Zhentarim guildmaster, and the
inn is where a strange figure first watched the party in Session 1.
