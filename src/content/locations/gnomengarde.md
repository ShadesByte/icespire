A colony of reclusive gnome inventors tucked into a river gorge on the coast,
ruled by King Korboz and Queen Gnerkli. The party came to deliver a dragon
warning and a trade contract; they left having survived a crossbow ballista,
disarmed a paranoid king's fears, and beaten two very real mimics in the
storage room.
