A colony of reclusive gnome inventors tucked into a waterfall gorge at the
southern end of the Sword Mountains, ruled by King Korboz and Queen Gnerkli. The party came to deliver a dragon
warning and a trade contract; they left having survived a crossbow ballista,
disarmed a paranoid king's fears, and beaten two very real mimics in the
storage room.
