The great peak that gives the campaign its name. Every rumor in the region —
orc raiders displaced from their territory, dwarves warned off their digs,
Harbin checking the sky before opening his door — points the same direction:
a white dragon has claimed the mountain. The party has been paid to spread
the warning. Sooner or later, the warning runs out.
